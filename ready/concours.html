What's an undercut?

under the rainbow
Seb and Max stop early on lap 19, followed by Charles on lap 20. Lewis leads, but his tires are worn. Cars on fresh tires are much faster.

When Lewis finally pits on lap 26, he comes out behind Seb, Charles, and Max. Seb and Max have undercut Lewis, and Seb has also undercut his own teammate Charles.

Seb and Max gained positions by stopping early, but it might cost them later. By the end of the race, their tires will be very old. Fortunately for them, 3 safety car periods make tire conservation easier.

How long does a pit stop take?

stoppage time
In 2019, a typical pit stop took 20-30 seconds, including time spent driving through the pit lane. Drive-through penalties can be used as an estimated lower bound for overall pit stop time.
stowe your roll
In 2014, the pit lane speed limit was lowered from 100kph to 80kph. (Some tracks already had speed limits below 100kph, but not Silverstone.)
yas we can
Abu Dhabi pit stop durations have low variance. Drivers apparently have no problems navigating its unusual pit exit tunnel.
paul ralentit
The redesigned Paul Ricard pit lane added around 5 seconds to a typical pit stop.
