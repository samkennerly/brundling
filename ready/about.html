Brundling About

brundle (verb)
brun·​dle ˈbrən-dᵊl
brundled; brundl​ing ˈbrən-dᵊl-iŋ
1. to persist with underpowered equipment against powerful opposition.
Whilst all but seven retired, Keke brundled his Fittipaldi over the melting tarmac to finish third.
