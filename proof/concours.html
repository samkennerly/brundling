What's an undercut?

under the rainbow
Seb and Max stop early on lap 19, followed by Charles on lap 20. Lewis leads, but his tires are worn. Cars on fresh tires are much faster.

When Lewis finally pits on lap 26, he comes out behind Seb, Charles, and Max. Seb and Max have undercut Lewis, and Seb has also undercut his own teammate Charles.

Seb and Max gained positions by stopping early, but it might cost them later. By the end of the race, their tires will be very old. Fortunately for them, 3 safety car periods make tire conservation easier.
